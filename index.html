HOW TO PLAY

Guess the word in 6 tries

Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word

The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

Example

W

O

R

D

Y

W and Y is in the word and in the correct spot
D is in the word and but in the wrong spot
O and R is not in the word in any spot
